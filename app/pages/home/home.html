The Pokestop Timer helps you farm Pokestops. Just simply activate the timer and it will remind you every X minutes to spin the Pokestop Circle. This way you don't have to look at your phone constantly. The normal cooldown of a Pokestop is 5 min. If you have enough items just reset the Timer
