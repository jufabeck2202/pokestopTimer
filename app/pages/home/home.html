The Pokestop Timer helps you farm Pokestops. Just simply activate the Timer and it will remind you every X minutes to spin the Pokestop Cirlce. This way you don't have to look at your Phone constantly. The normal cooldown of a Pokestop is 5 min. If you have enough Items just Reset the Timer
